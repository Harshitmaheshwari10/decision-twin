The problem

Financial decisions are still made in the dark.

CFOs, individual investors, and founders make their biggest financial calls the same way they always have — on personal judgment, a spreadsheet, and whatever data happened to be on hand. There is no system that tests a decision's downstream effects before the money moves.

The technology to do better has existed for a while. What's held it back is trust. Handing sensitive financial data to a shared AI model — one you don't control and can't fully see inside — is a risk most institutions and individuals aren't willing to take.

01

The trust gap

The solution

A digital twin of your finances, run privately.

Twin is a multi-agent AI platform that runs as its own encrypted, isolated instance for every individual, startup, and enterprise. It builds a live twin of your finances, answers questions in plain language, and simulates every major decision before it's finalised.

Live twin

A model that mirrors reality

The twin tracks income, burn, treasury, or whatever matters for your kind of finances — continuously, not as a quarterly snapshot.

Ask, don't dig

Grounded in your real data

A conversational agent, powered by retrieval-augmented generation, answers financial questions using your actual numbers — never a generic response.

Private by design

Your data never leaves home

Every user gets a dedicated, encrypted instance. Nothing is pooled, shared, or used to train anything outside your walls.

The agent team

Six specialists. One job each.

Rather than a swarm of micro-agents, Twin groups the work into six clear roles, each reporting back to the orchestrator.

Understand

Pulls in and cleans up the user's financial data.

Watch

Constantly checks for risk — low cash, bad debt, fraud, FX exposure.

Simulate

Runs "what-if" tests on the digital twin.

Recommend

Picks the best option based on the simulation results.

Teach

Explains the recommendation in plain language.

Check

Confirms it's compliant, and explains why the call was made.

Trust & security

Your instance. Your data. Nobody else's.

The reason financial AI hasn't been adopted more widely isn't capability — it's trust. Twin is built around that constraint, not in spite of it.

Isolated by default

Every individual, startup, and enterprise runs on its own independent instance — never a shared model, never pooled data.

Encrypted end to end

Financial data is encrypted at rest and in transit, inside a boundary the user controls.

Compliance built in

The Check agent verifies every recommendation against relevant regulation before it reaches you — and explains why.

Evidence, not guesses

Every recommendation is backed by a simulation on your own data, not a generic model completion.
